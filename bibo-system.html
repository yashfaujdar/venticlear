BIBO SYSTEM

Safe, Contained Solutions for Hazardous Environments

Product Overview

BIBO (Bag-In/Bag-Out) Systems provide the ultimate in safe filter handling for hazardous environments. These systems allow for complete containment during filter changes, preventing exposure to dangerous contaminants. Designed for maximum safety and zero contamination risk, BIBO systems are essential for facilities handling toxic, radioactive, or highly infectious materials.
