PRE FILTER

The First Line of Defense Against Airborne Contaminants

Product Overview

Pre filters serve as the initial stage of filtration in HVAC systems, capturing larger particles such as dust, pollen, and dirt. By preventing these contaminants from reaching finer filters, they enhance efficiency and extend the lifespan of HEPA and fine filters. Commonly used in HVAC systems, food processing plants, malls, and industrial facilities, pre filters help maintain clean air while ensuring optimal system performance and energy efficiency.
